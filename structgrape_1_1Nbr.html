template<typename VID_T, typename EDATA_T>
struct grape::Nbr< VID_T, EDATA_T >

A neighbor of a vertex in the graph.

Assume an edge, vertex_a –(edge_data)--> vertex_b. a <Nbr> of vertex_a stores <Vertex> b and the edge_data.
